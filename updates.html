New Paper! Evaluating satellite and modeled lake surface water temperature across the contiguous United States

Authors: Blake A Schaeffer, Hannah Ferriby, Wilson Salls, Natalie Reynolds, Jeffrey W Hollister, Betty Kreakie, Stephen Shivers, Brent Johnson, Olivia Cronin-Golomb, Kate Meyers, Maxwell Beal

Publication Research
March 2026

At the EPA, I contributed to the valdiation of a U.S. water temperature product for Sentinel-3 resolved lakes. Using a random forest approach, Landsat ARD surface temperature, and PRISM air temperature, water temperature can be estimated quickly and accurately. This product is used in the pixel scale forecasting work highlighted below! Access here
