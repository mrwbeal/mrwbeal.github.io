Max Beal

Postdoctoral Researcher · NASA Ocean Ecology Lab

My work takes a water systems approach to water quality monitoring and forecasting. My interests lie at the intersection of remote sensing, hydroclimatology, machine learning, and aquatic science.

Currently Working On

At NASA I am developing methods to merge ocean color data from multiple satellite platforms to generate a long term radiometry and chlorophyll-a timeseries for the coastal U.S.
