Research

Research Themes

My research focuses on forecasting and remote sensing of water quality. I develop multi-satellite water quality products, investigate hydroclimate-water quality relationships, and build forecasting systems to predict water quality extremes from weeks to seasons.

Focus Area 01

Algae Forecasting and Early Warning Systems

Harmful algal bloom forecasts that extend management lead time from days to seasons.

I develop forecasting systems that extend decision-making horizons for water managers from days to seasons. This work focuses on harmful algal blooms, where earlier warning can inform monitoring strategies, beach closings, and risk communication.

One of my projects is extending harmful algae forecasts in space. With satellite-based chlorophyll observations, forecasts can be developed at the pixel-scale.

Focus Area 02

Remote Sensing of Water Quality

Satellite-enabled retrievals of water quality indicators linked to field observations.

I combine satellite imagery with in-situ monitoring to retrieve algal pigments and dissolved oxygen, enabling scalable lake-wide water quality assessment. This work integrates Sentinel-2 and Sentinel-3 observations with machine learning models for robust, transferable workflows.

At NASA, I am developing methods to merge ocean color data from multiple satellite platforms to generate a long term radiometry and chlorophyll-a timeseries for the coastal U.S.

Focus Area 03

Hydroclimatology and Ecosystem Extremes

Other research projects focused around hydroclimatology, forecasting, and ecological extremes.

Hydroclimatology and subseasonal-to-seasonal forecasting can be applied to a wide variety of water- and ecology-related extremes. Below are some of my additional projects in this area.

Dengue fever is a mosquito-borne viral disease rapidly creating a significant global public health burden, particularly in urban areas of tropical and sub-tropical countries. Using climate variables to predict dengue cases at longer, weeks to months, lead times may help improve preparation for dengue outbreaks.
